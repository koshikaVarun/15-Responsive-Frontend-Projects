THE WAR

The 2023 Israel-Hamas war is an ongoing armed conflict between Israel and Hamas-led Palestinian militant groups. The war began on October 7, 2023. The conflict has been taking place in and around the Gaza Strip, the West Bank, and the Israel-Lebanon border. On October 7, Hamas announced the start of "Operation Al-Aqs Flood". Hamas claimed to have fired over 5,000 rockets from the Gaza Strip into Israel within 20 minutes. Israeli sources reported that at least 3,000 projectiles had been launched from Gaza. Hamas gunmen also killed 1,200 people and took about 240 hostages. Israeli commanders have said they are working to remove Hamas from power and free more than 100 hostages. They are also concerned that U.S. support will dry up if the war drags on.
